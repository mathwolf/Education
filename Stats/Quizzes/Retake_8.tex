\question[10]
We conduct and experiment by drawing a card from a deck and then flipping a coin.
\begin{parts}
\part
What is the probability that the card is a queen?
\vspace{\stretch{2}}

\part
What is the probability that the coin shows heads?
\vspace{\stretch{2}}

\part
What is the probability of drawing a queen and getting heads?
\vspace{\stretch{2}}

\part
What is the probability of drawing a queen or getting heads?

\vspace{\stretch{3}}

\end{parts}

\question[10]
An urn contains 4 red chips and 2 green chips.  Two chips are drawn from the jar.
\begin{parts}
\part
What is the probability that the first chip is red?

\vspace{\stretch{2}}

\part
If the first chip is red, what is the probability that the second chip is green?

\vspace{\stretch{2}}

\part
What is the probability of getting two green chips?

\vspace{\stretch{2}}

\part
What is the probability that the second chip is green?

\vspace{\stretch{3}}

\end{parts}
